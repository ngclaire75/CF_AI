Privacy & Security

Your data is treated like it's our own

We built Intel Web Security with privacy-first principles from day one. Here's exactly how we protect your information.

Encrypted at rest & in transit

All your data is encrypted with AES-256 at rest and TLS 1.3 in transit. Nobody can read your security data — not even us.

Read-only access, always

Intel Web Security monitors your systems without ever writing to them. We observe and protect — we never modify your data or codebase.

No credentials stored

We never store API keys, passwords, or tokens. Authentication uses short-lived tokens that expire after each session ends.

Your data is never shared

We will never sell, share, or use your security data for any purpose other than providing the platform to you. Full stop.

Delete your data anytime

Request a full data deletion at any time from your account settings. We purge everything — logs, events, scan results — within 48 hours.

99.9% uptime commitment

Your protection never sleeps. Our infrastructure is built for high availability with automatic failover — because security can't have downtime.
